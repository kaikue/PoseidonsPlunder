Poseidon's Plunder

by: Eric Fang (efang), Edward Terry (eterry), I-Chen Jwo (ijwo), Kai Kuehner (kkuehner)

Underwater capture-the-flag game.

Two teams are on an arena. Each team tries to swim to the other side and grab their opponents' treasure, then bring it back to their base.
